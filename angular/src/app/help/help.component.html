Introduction

This is the introduction page of Anon Square. The word "anon" is short for "anonymous".

No user can trace other user's post history or information except the admin.

Mark the post you're interested in so you'll not lose it.

You can change your nickname anytime you want, or just use 'Anonymous' when sending a new post.

The 'Private' checkbox keeps the post secret so other users can't see it or search it.

The only potential risk of using a fake email is you can't reset your password through it.
